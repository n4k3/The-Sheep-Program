Yearly Budget problem

In a hospital, we can find three areas: Urgencies, Pediatric and Traumatology. The yearly budget is distributed: Urgencies (37%), Pediatric (42%) and Traumatology (21%). Get the amount of money that each section will receive from a given budget.

Calculating the salary

Do a flowchart that can calculate the yearly salary of a person that earns $40000 per year in accordance of the following criteria: If employee is working more than 10 years, will apply an increment of 10%. If employee is working less than 10 years but more than 5 years, will apply an increment of 7%. If employee is working less than 5 years but more than 3 years, will apply an increment of 5%. If employee is working less than 3 years, will apply an increment of 3%.
